Snapchat launched the stories format in 2013, since then it has been adopted by a great number of platforms. In 2019 Bloomberg reported that Instagram had brought in $20 billion in advertising revenues, in great part thanks to this format.

During 2020-2021, news websites adopted Google's web stories format, and Softonic decided to experiment with it.

<ProjectImage src="/images/projects/webstories/stories-samples.jpg" alt="Web stories samples" />

## Project Goals

The team aimed to promote CPA and CPI products through a new channel, increase engagement, and attract mobile organic traffic via Google Discover. We followed Lean methodologies over roughly two months to test content performance and assess tools.

## My Role

Product Designer — Research, Prototyping, Testing, Stories Design, Writing & Content Creation. I studied competitor implementations, evaluated creation tools, designed components, created surveys, and analyzed data.

## First Iteration

Testing focused exclusively on mobile. Initial experiments used dummy stories on program pages with typeform surveys. Two variants tested: contextual reviews and editorial topics. Results showed CTRs below 2%, indicating low transactional-phase interest. Survey feedback revealed interest in user-generated video reviews and gaming content.

<ProjectImage src="/images/projects/webstories/stories-news.png" alt="Stories news variant" />

## Second Iteration

Testing moved to category pages (discovery phase). Editorial and gaming-specific variants were tested. CTRs averaged around 8%, with Minecraft and Roblox stories performing exceptionally well.

<ProjectImage src="/images/projects/webstories/stories-gaming-news.png" alt="Gaming news stories" />

<ProjectImage src="/images/projects/webstories/stories-gaming.png" alt="Gaming stories" />

## Third Iteration

Full infrastructure implementation allowed testing actual functionality. Campaigns focused on Mobile Legends and Lords Mobile with revenue-share in-app purchase programs. Content was SEO-optimized for Google Discover. The team selected makestories.io as their platform and created reusable templates plus tutorial stories.

## Conclusions & Learnings

This project allowed creative exploration with gradients and bold color palettes not possible in day-to-day work. It provided opportunity to experiment with modern design trends and explore print design.
