During the winter of 2014 the Flappy Bird game went viral. At the time I was working as a freelance web developer and designer, and had some free time that summer to pursue mobile game development.

## The Project

As a first-time mobile game creator with online tutorial experience, I was inspired by Catalan independence news and the wave of Flappy Bird clones. I decided to build a parody game to learn Unity and app publishing. Development took approximately one week to get to Play Store readiness. I created all visual assets, edited sound effects, and coded the game.

The game features former Catalan president Artur Mas as protagonist escaping Spain — an ironic view of the Catalan independence desires. As a curiosity, the game is intentionally frustrating and never ends, mirroring Spanish political conflicts.

## Reception

The game was featured in Catalan news outlets, radio, and local television. It's still available on the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.adriacompte.flapimas).
